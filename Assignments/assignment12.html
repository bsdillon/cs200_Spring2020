Assignment #12 - AI Questions

Purpose
Evaluate the concept of "intelligence" as a precursor to discussion of Artificial Intelligence.
Instructions
Answer the following questions as paragraph-long responses on a single web page.
  1. Scientists thrive on definitions that can help them tell one thing from another. What are the characteristics of life? Give at least three tests to determine life by its characteristics.
  2. Give a counter example to each of your characteristics. Why is it hard to define life?
  3. What are the characteristics of intelligence? Give at least three examples.
  4. Give a counter example to each of your characteristics. Why is it hard to define intelligence?
  5. If a human makes a mistake, is that evidence of intelligence or lack of intelligence? Discuss.
  6. If a machine does a job as well as a human, but makes a mistake is that evidence of intelligence or lack of intelligence? Discuss.
  7. Submit the link to the page in Canvas.
