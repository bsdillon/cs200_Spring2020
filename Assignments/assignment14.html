Assignment #14 - Language of Choice: Hello World

Purpose
Explore programming in another language
Instructions
  1. Watch this video about simple vs complicated programming languages.
  2. Write a paragraph describing your family using only single syllable words. Minimum of 100 words. What are the limitations of a low level language? What does the speaker say about the advantages and limitations?
  3. Write a paragraph describing your dream job using whatever words you want. Minimum of 100 words. The paragraph must be marked as follows. What are the limitations of a high-level language with support structure? What does the speaker say about the advantages and limitations?
    • Make each article (a, an, the) bold.
    • Highlight each noun in blue.
    • Highlight each verb in red.
    • Highlight each preposition in yellow.
    • Underline each prepositional phrase in this way.
  4. Select a programming language other that HTML/CSS/JavaScript.
  5. Find documentation on that language and setup the development environment on your computer.
  6. Write the traditional "Hello World program in this language".
  7. Get a copy of your code in that language. Get a screen capture of the output in whatever form. Write a paragraph or more with your impressions of the new language.
  8. Put all these elements in a page. Test to make sure the buttons work and resolve any bugs.
  9. Submit the link to the page in Canvas.
