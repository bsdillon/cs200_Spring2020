GoldField Links

GoldField was created as a free API for beginning CS students. It does most of the heavy lifting and allows students to focus on the piece of programming that they are learning. It is object-oriented with sounds, icons, and room for immediate extension.

Trig Education Links

The educational links are part of a series of math-literacy efforts that integrate with CS-literacy. They are designed to help a beginner student achieve a reasonable standard before starting real project work as art of Expeditionary Dahlgren. This set focuses on trig.

  1. X axis and 1-D distances formula
  2. Y axis
  3. Cartesian plane and quadrants
  4. Vectors and slope
  5. Vector magnitude, Pythagorean theorem, 2-D distance
  6. Sin, Cos, Tan on a triangle/vector
  7. Unit circle, +/- θ
  8. Programming: y-inversion
  9. Radians
  10. Angle and magnitude from component vectors
  11. Programming: Sensing and control structures
  12. Programming: Chase Scene
  13. Programming: Acting on a time interval
  14. Velocity components
  15. Polar/rectangular coordinate
