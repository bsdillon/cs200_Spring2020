Homework 10 - Computer Specification

This homework will be used to investigate the specifications of your personal computer. You may pick any computer at home, work, or school. Record the required information and publish it. If you have any trouble with these instructions, you are free to look up the stats in other ways.

  1. Use speed test to get statistics on your network connection. Use your home internet connection for this test. Record the following data:
    • Download speed in Megabits per second (Mbps)
    • Upload speed in Megabits per second (Mbps)
  2. Call your internet service provider and ask them what their quality of service standards are for your home. See if they match your findings above.
  3. Verizon Fios makes a claim that if your service has slower uploads it is "half fast". Do you have FIOS? Do you have "half fast" service? What do you think of this problem? Write a paragraph on this topic in your response.
  4. If you have a windows system, hit the windows button and type "PC". Right click on the MyPC icon and select "Properties".
    If you have an apple system, click the Apple icon in the top-left corner of your Mac. From the drop-down menu, pick "About This Mac".
    Record these specs for processor and RAM (you may need to look up the chip).
    • Number of cores
    • Total processor speed
    • 32- or 64-bit architecture
    • RAM size
  5. If you have windows machine, open up windows explorer click on "This PC" in the left panel. Scroll down on the right panel so you can see each drive.
    If you have an apple system, click the Apple icon in the top-left corner of your Mac. From the drop-down menu, pick "About This Mac".
    Record the following data for each drive.
    • Available space
    • Total space
  6. Read the specifications for a CRAY-2. This was the biggest and best computer of the 1980s. In Jurrasic Park they had 3 to run through the DNA of the dinosaurs. This was a serious, top-flight machine. Record the same information that you did for your personal computer.
  7. Write a paragraph comparing your computer with the CRAY-2.
  8. Put all these elements in a page. Test to make sure the buttons work as expected and resolve any bugs.
